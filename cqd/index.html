QUANTUM DOTS: SYNTHESIS AND STUDY

WHAT ARE QUANTUM DOTS?

Quantum Dots are nanoparticles that emit light when electricity or light falls on them.

For this project, I led a group of three members to synthesize and study properties of Carbon and Graphene Quantum dots. I was responsible for poster design. Our team won the best poster award at the ThinkNano Symposium 2016, Indian Institute of Science

Team Members: Tanuja Sawant, Samyukta Ramnath, George Joseph

Duration: 1 week
